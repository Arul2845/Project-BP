Chapter Two

Moments I'll Never Forget

Three memories I secretly hold closer than you'll ever know.

7:00 AM · First Temple Date · Golden Morning Silence
Our Beginning
🛕

The Morning I Never Forgot

It was 7 in the morning, our very first temple date. The bells were ringing softly, incense filled the air, and the sunlight made everything feel peaceful. I still remember how small and innocent you looked that day — that little girl still lives in my heart. We walked together quietly, holding emotions we didn’t know how to express, and hand in hand, we took our very first official photo together. Some memories never fade, and this will always be one of them.

8:15 PM · CSC Ending Day · Rainy Evening Memories
Year Two
🚌

The CSC Return Journey

I still remember waiting there for almost half an hour, just to make sure I wouldn’t miss you. And when you finally came, somehow the whole day felt brighter. We laughed, ate ice cream, shared pani poori, and spent those little moments together after your CSC ended with your friends. Looking back now, I miss those simple happy days more than anything.

4:30 PM · Near College Shop · Sweet Little Moments
Year Three
🍹

Falooda Near College

Those days near the small shop outside your college still feel special to me. We would sit there, eat falooda, laugh at random things, and forget about the world for a while. Even on our anniversary, we met during your lunch break and spent those little moments together so happily. Simple days, but they became some of the most beautiful memories of my life.
