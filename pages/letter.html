28th May 2025

Dear Pretty,

I don't always say what I feel out loud — but today I want you to read every word.

You came into my life and made it softer, brighter, warmer. The kovil, the falooda afternoons, every little moment — I carry them all.

You deserve every happiness this world has.

"And even though we are apart now — I will always, always wish for you to be happy. That is my promise. That is my prayer for you. Always."

Today you turn 21. I hope it feels like the beginning of everything beautiful you deserve. Go be brilliant, Pretty. Go be loved — the way you always should have been.

Always. — Arul 💕
